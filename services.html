Services that we Provide

Afriwave Engineering also installs Generator sets, Transformers, Electrical services design and supply of 3-phase electricity from ECG mains to Cell Sites. Afriwave Engineering offers to Network Operators, a complete solution to all their needs as far as network rollout is concerned. One other area that we operate is site maintenance. We can perform routine site maintenance like periodic checks on Gen sets, Air-conditioners and the refueling of Gen sets with diesel as and when it is required.

Rectifier and DC Power Installation

Our team understands that the power for your network is crucial to your success. We are experts with deep experience in designing, deploying and supporting integrated power solutions that deliver optimal results.

Site Build and Restoration

Site building is one key area where we specialize in to provide quality network to all. We also restore old sites to current standard in the network industry to enhance smart living.
